# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with the Recruiting Platform in minutes! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to start recruiting effectively.

## Step 1: Set Up Your Account (5 minutes)

### Create Your Organization

1. **Sign up** at [platform.com/signup](https://platform.com/signup)
2. **Verify your email** by clicking the link sent to your inbox
3. **Complete your profile**:
   - Organization name
   - Industry
   - Company size
   - Time zone
4. **Invite team members** (optional, can be done later)

### Configure Basic Settings

Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Organization**:

1. **Branding**: Upload your company logo
2. **Locations**: Add your office locations
3. **Departments**: Set up your organizational structure
4. **Email**: Connect your email account (Gmail or Outlook)

## Step 2: Create Your First Job (10 minutes)

### Post a Job Opening

1. Click **Jobs** in the main navigation
2. Click **+ New Job** button
3. Fill in the job details:

```
Title: Senior Software Engineer
Department: Engineering
Location: San Francisco, CA (Remote OK)
Employment Type: Full-time
Salary Range: $120,000 - $180,000

Description:
We're looking for an experienced software engineer to join our growing team...

Requirements:
- 5+ years of software development experience
- Strong knowledge of JavaScript, React, Node.js
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS/GCP)
- Excellent communication skills
```

4. **Set up pipeline stages**:
   - Applied
   - Phone Screen
   - Technical Interview
   - Final Interview
   - Offer
   - Hired

5. **Configure interview plan** (optional):
   - Add interview stages
   - Assign interviewers
   - Create scorecards

6. Click **Save as Draft** or **Publish**

### Publish to Job Boards

1. Open your job
2. Click **Post to Job Boards**
3. Select boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor)
4. Review and confirm
5. Click **Publish**

## Step 3: Add Your First Candidate (5 minutes)

### Manual Entry

1. Click **Candidates** in the main navigation
2. Click **+ New Candidate**
3. Enter candidate information:
   - Name and email (required)
   - Phone number
   - Current title and company
   - LinkedIn profile
   - Skills/tags

4. **Upload resume** (optional):
   - Drag and drop or click to upload
   - AI will automatically parse the resume
   - Review and confirm extracted data

5. Click **Save**

### Using Chrome Extension

1. Install the [Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore)
2. Navigate to a LinkedIn profile
3. Click the extension icon
4. Click **Save to Platform**
5. Select job or talent pool
6. Candidate is automatically added!

## Step 4: Create an Application (3 minutes)

### Link Candidate to Job

1. Open the candidate profile
2. Click **+ Add to Job**
3. Select the job
4. Choose initial stage (usually "Applied")
5. Add source information
6. Click **Create Application**

### View in Pipeline

1. Navigate to **Jobs** ‚Üí Select your job
2. Click **Pipeline** tab
3. See your candidate in the kanban board
4. Drag and drop to move between stages

## Step 5: Schedule an Interview (10 minutes)

### Using Calendar Integration

1. Open the application
2. Click **Schedule Interview**
3. Select interview type (Phone, Video, Onsite)
4. Choose interviewers
5. Select date and time
6. Add meeting details (Zoom link, phone number, etc.)
7. Click **Send Invites**

### Using Self-Service Scheduling

1. Open the application
2. Click **Generate Scheduling Link**
3. Select available time slots
4. Copy the link
5. Send to candidate via email
6. Candidate selects their preferred time
7. Interview is automatically scheduled!

## Step 6: Collect Feedback (5 minutes)

### After the Interview

1. Navigate to **Interviews**
2. Find the completed interview
3. Click **Submit Feedback**
4. Fill out the scorecard:
   - Overall rating (1-5 stars)
   - Decision (Strong Yes, Yes, Neutral, No, Strong No)
   - Strengths and concerns
   - Attribute ratings
5. Click **Submit**

### View Feedback

1. Open the application
2. Click **Interviews** tab
3. See all feedback from interviewers
4. Make hiring decision based on feedback

## Step 7: Send an Offer (10 minutes)

### Create Offer

1. Open the application
2. Click **Create Offer**
3. Fill in offer details:
   - Salary: $150,000
   - Bonus: $20,000
   - Equity: 0.1%
   - Start date: January 1, 2026
4. Select offer template
5. Click **Generate Offer Letter**

### Send for Approval

1. Click **Request Approval**
2. Select approvers (Hiring Manager, Finance, etc.)
3. Add notes
4. Click **Send**

### Send to Candidate

Once approved:
1. Click **Send Offer**
2. Select e-signature provider (DocuSign)
3. Review offer letter
4. Click **Send for Signature**
5. Candidate receives email with signing link

## Step 8: Track Analytics (5 minutes)

### View Dashboard

1. Navigate to **Analytics**
2. Select **Recruiting Funnel** dashboard
3. View key metrics:
   - Applications this month
   - Conversion rates by stage
   - Time to fill
   - Offer acceptance rate

### Create Custom Report

1. Click **Reports** ‚Üí **+ New Report**
2. Select data source (Applications, Candidates, etc.)
3. Choose columns and filters
4. Add visualizations
5. Click **Generate Report**
6. Export as PDF or Excel

## Tips for Success

### 1. Set Up Your Pipeline
Customize pipeline stages to match your hiring process. Common stages:
- Applied ‚Üí Phone Screen ‚Üí Technical ‚Üí Final ‚Üí Offer ‚Üí Hired

### 2. Use Templates
Create email templates for common communications:
- Application confirmation
- Interview invitation
- Rejection (with feedback)
- Offer letter

### 3. Leverage AI
Let AI do the heavy lifting:
- Parse resumes automatically
- Calculate match scores
- Generate personalized emails
- Predict time to fill

### 4. Collaborate Effectively
- Use @mentions in comments
- Share feedback promptly
- Keep notes updated
- Tag candidates appropriately

### 5. Track Metrics
Monitor these key metrics:
- Time to fill
- Source effectiveness
- Conversion rates
- Candidate satisfaction (NPS)

- Try manual sync
